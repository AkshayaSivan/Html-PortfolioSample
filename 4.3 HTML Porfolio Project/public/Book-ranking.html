The Best Books According to Akshaya

My top 3 books of all-time.

Six of crows duology

The entire heist and plot twists were done so well that gives you a highly satisfying ending.It is a fantasy genre book suitable for Young adults .

The Hunger games

This was the series that set off the trend for dystopian novels and was one of the major sellers.It is the perfect series for beginners to get started

The Mistborn Trilogy

This series by Brandon Sanderson elaborately explains all of the geography and working of allomancy very well. The last 100 pages of the third book brought together every missing piece to give us an incredible ending.
